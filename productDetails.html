Your journey starts here.

Hence, Piano Store was born in 2021 and the first location was on Bucharest. This family owned and operated, small business piano store, began to thrive and gain a reputation for the quality pianos, competitive pricing and the very heartfelt customer service Geroge gave to his customers.

Further, he launched a non-profit that helps place donated pianos into the hands of organizations and individuals who could not otherwise afford it. George is also known for his genuine support and generosity through financial donations and providing pianos for many local community events.

Realizing his customers were nationwide, George has developed an online platform making it easier for customers to find the perfect piano for them using his web platforms through social media and here, on his customer-focused website!
